root:

index.js - what you use to initiate your app
node_modules - where the packages live
package.json - tells you what the dependencies are for you project
bin - (binary files) this is where the code goes - the core of our project. Also called src, app, whatever
test(s) - all my js test files. So you can run your tests all at once
